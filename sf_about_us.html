Meet Our Team

We have a dedicated team committed to providing you with high-quality, reliable products and services.

With extensive global and local knowledge, we have the expertise to deliver a value added solution tailored to your needs. Our team is spread across the nation and can easily meet in person or online.

John Sheep

Johns passion is around eco diveristy and breeding management.

John has spent many years at the European Farming Institute and specialises in land, nutrition, and breed development in Sheep.

Tim Merino

Tim specialises in bio diversity and land governance.

He has extensive experience in the NZ and Australian Sheep industry having held a number of roles with the Ministry of Primary Industries (MPI) and the Victorian State Sheep and Beef Department.

Adam Romney

Adam is our breeding specialist.

After graduating from Lincoln University with a BSc majoring in Agricture), John spent 5 years in the Netherlands completing an internship at the Dutch Ovine Research Institute.

Paula Wool

Paula is our pasture and land management specialist.

Paula has spent 5 years in farm breeding development roles in Europe and a further 5 years in the Bio tech industry specialising in Sheep breeding in Germany.
